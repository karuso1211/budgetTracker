iPhone Setup

Install it like a native app

1
Open in Safari
Make sure you're using Safari on your iPhone — it's the only browser that supports PWA installation on iOS.
2
Tap the Share button
Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).
3
Add to Home Screen
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen", then tap "Add" in the top right corner.
4
Launch & use offline
Open it from your Home Screen like any app. It runs full-screen and works without internet after the first load.
